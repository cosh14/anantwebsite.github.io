WHY SPONSOR US?

Development of a nanosatellite at a university level is a very ambitious and prestigious project for any institute. The amalgamation of new and emerging technologies, research and applications of almost all the different domains of various engineering and science disciplines call for a great need of guidance and expertise at every step of the project. Moreover continued research, procurement of high precision equipments, construction of highly controlled working environment and testing labs require a strong financial base for the project.

Your support will fuel the team's drive to excellence and global pre-eminence. Undoubtedly, a project of this stature and magnitude requires great deal of planning and hard work but, together we can take Team Anant to "infinity and beyond".
